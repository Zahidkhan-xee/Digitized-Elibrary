ABOUT LIBRARY.

The college library is managed by a qualified Chief Librarian assisted by a good number of other library staff. 

The Reference Section of the Library is quite rich catering to the needs of the faculty and the students, especially the advanced learners. The Library has other facilities like reprography. Computer cataloging of the library is presently in process. The library works for about seven hours a day and the number of working days is about 300 / year. The upgrading and updating of the Library is done regularly as per the requirements of the curriculum and every effort is made to facilitate easy access of students to various sections of the Library. A separate room stands earmarked as Reading Room wherein a number of local, national, international Magazines / journals besides newspapers are available for the benefit of the students and the teaching / non- teaching staff. The Library also has a separate corner for displaying the college publications, write-ups of students in local / national dailies and also the college magazine. A separate Library Block is presently under construction and after its completion the existing library facility is expected to get a big boost by way of computerizing of the library, establishing an Audio / Video library, providing an internet facility and other such services.

Responsibilities of the Library Committee:

  • The committee formulates development plans and recommends facilities for implementation.
  • The committee gives guidelines for the procurement of costly books, internet journals, hardware and software necessary for the library.
  • The committee ensures the optimal use of library facility by staff and students.
  • The committee organizes book fairs as well as visits to national and international book fairs.
  • It ensures the availability of latest syllabi in the library.
  • It takes up remedial actions towards the grievances expressed by the students and staff.
