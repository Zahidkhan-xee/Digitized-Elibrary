Why To Digitized Library?

The Electronic Library or the E-Library is designed and maintained by the department of Computer Science specially meant for the students and Professors of the college so they can study and relish the book electronically - read as they go without the hinderance of issuence of books through the library process. The E-Library Contains over more than 500 Electonic Books (PDF)s which are stored on the server. To enjoy the free reading of books you are required to Login using your University Registeration Number and for the College faculity they are required to Sign In Using their IDs.
